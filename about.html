About filobearo!

filobearo is a small bear made from the freshest  pastry dough, hence the name filo! (A play on phyllo dough!) filo's goal is to travel the world, make new friends, and create a welcoming space for LGBTQ+ in the streetwear industry!

filo's design started off as an instagram filler on our CEO's profile. He fell in love with the design and decided to spread that love for him through fashion!

About the creator!

Richie is filobearo's designer/CEO/product creator! Richie hand makes our earrings, rugs, and cyanotype shirts! 

Richie is a part of the LGBTQ+ community in Edmonton. Inspired by streatwear, he wanted to create something both cutesy, positive, and accepting of everyone! Encouraging happiness as much as filobearo can!
